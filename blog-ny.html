Project Exhibation at NewYork Maker Faire

Sep 10, 2016

We were invited to showcase our project at New York Maker Faire.

NY Maker Faire is one of the most prestigiouse exhibitions in the worlds.Makers from all over the world go there and showcase their project.

In 2016 our one of the projects Smart Wheelchair which is a low cost wheal chair controlled by android app got selected .Unfortunatly our US visa was rejected and we could not go there to exhibit the project.

NY Maker faire commetee sent a token of appreciation from NY also they featured our project in Maker Faire magazine and added us as maker in their website.
